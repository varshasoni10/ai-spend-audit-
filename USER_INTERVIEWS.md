# User Interviews

## Interview 1: "S", CTO of a Series A DevTools Startup
- **Quote 1**: "We just pay the OpenAI API bill every month via auto-pay. I honestly have no idea who has a ChatGPT Plus license vs who is using the API."
- **Quote 2**: "If you told me I could save $500 a month, I'd probably ignore it. If you told me I could save $5,000 a year without changing my team's workflow, I'd take the meeting."
- **Quote 3**: "I hate giving my email before I see the result. If a tool gates the result, I just close the tab."
- **Surprising thing**: He valued the *time savings* of not having to audit the tools himself higher than the actual *dollar savings*.
- **Impact on design**: Reinforced the rule: show the value FIRST, then ask for the email. Also made me emphasize "Annual Savings" in giant text on the hero results section because monthly savings look too small.

## Interview 2: "M", Founder of a Bootstrapped SaaS (5 people)
- **Quote 1**: "We all use Cursor. We're on the Business plan because I thought we needed it for privacy."
- **Quote 2**: "I don't have a finance guy. I am the finance guy."
- **Quote 3**: "Wait, what's Credex?"
- **Surprising thing**: Many founders don't realize that lower-tier plans (like Pro vs Business) often have the exact same capabilities, just missing centralized billing or SSO which a 5-person team doesn't need anyway.
- **Impact on design**: The tool needs to explicitly state *why* they can downgrade. "You only have 5 users, you don't need SSO. Downgrade to Pro."

## Interview 3: "A", VP of Engineering at a Series B Startup
- **Quote 1**: "My CFO literally slacked me yesterday asking why our Anthropic bill spiked."
- **Quote 2**: "I don't want a consultation unless I know exactly what you're selling."
- **Quote 3**: "I'd share this with my founder group chat if it actually works."
- **Surprising thing**: She was actively defensive about sales calls. The CTA for Credex needs to be extremely soft and value-driven, not "Book a Demo".
- **Impact on design**: Changed the final CTA from "Talk to Sales" to "See how Credex can capture these savings automatically."
