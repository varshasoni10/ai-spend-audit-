# Unit Economics

## Value of a Converted Lead
Assuming Credex sells discounted AI credits with an average contract value (ACV) of $10,000 and takes a 15% margin, the Gross Profit per customer is $1,500.
If the lifetime of a customer is 2 years, LTV = $3,000.
A high-intent lead (someone who already saw they have >$500/mo in savings) has an estimated 10% close rate.
Therefore, the value of a high-intent lead to Credex is **$300**.

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Hacker News / Organic Social**: $0 hard cost, just time. CAC = $0.
- **Cold Email**: ~$0.05 per email (data enrichment + sending). With a 1% conversion to lead, CAC = $5 per lead.
- **Paid Ads (Future)**: Assuming $2 CPC and 5% form completion, 20% lead capture = $200 per lead. (Still profitable against $300 value, but organic is better).

## Conversion Funnel Targets
- 1000 Landing Page Visitors
- 300 Audits Completed (30%)
- 60 Emails Captured (20% of audits)
- 15 Credex Consultations Booked (25% of captured emails)
- 1.5 Credit Purchases (10% close rate on consultations)

## Path to $1M ARR
To generate $1M ARR (assuming $10k ACV), Credex needs 100 new customers.
At a 10% close rate from consultation, we need 1,000 consultations.
At a 25% booking rate from captured emails, we need 4,000 emails.
At a 20% capture rate from audits, we need 20,000 completed audits.
At a 30% completion rate, we need **66,666 unique visitors** over 18 months.
This is highly achievable if the tool becomes the standard "benchmark your spend" link shared in tech communities.
